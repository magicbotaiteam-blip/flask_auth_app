What's an AI Agent?

Think of an AI agent as a smart assistant that doesn't just talk — it acts. You tell it what to do in plain English, and it uses tools to get things done:

Search the Web

Look up prices, news, weather, flights — anything online.

Send Emails

Compose and send reports, alerts, or daily digests automatically.

Analyze Data

Check stock prices, compute metrics, summarize trends.

Run on a Schedule

Do the same task every morning, every hour, or every Monday.

What's a Scheduled Task (Cron)?

A cron job is simply a task that runs automatically at a specific time. Instead of manually checking something every day, you set it once and forget it.

✅ Every morning 8:00 AM

"Check grocery deals and email me the best prices"

✅ Every trading day 9:00 AM

"Run stock screener and post top 3 stocks to my group"

✅ Every Monday 10:00 AM

"Search for cruise deals and send the best ones"

✅ Every day 9:00 AM

"Send today's weather forecast to my family group"

Real Things AI Agents Do Every Day

These are actual cron jobs running right now. Simple prompts, powerful results.

Every day 8:00 AM Grocery Shopping

Church Grocery Deal Scanner

Every morning at 8 AM, the agent checks 6 grocery stores (Costco, Walmart, ALDI, Lidl, Great Wall, H-Mart) for deals on chicken thighs, eggs, pork belly, and rice. If prices are below a threshold, it emails the results to the church coordinators.

Prompt (what you tell the bot):
# Your daily grocery scanner to find the best bulk prices
# Only reports when prices are genuinely good — no noise if nothing's on sale

Search weekly ads for these items in Fairfax, VA:
• Chicken thighs < $2.50/lb
• Eggs (5 dozen) < $12
• Pork belly < $3.50/lb
• Rice (25lb) < $20

Check: Costco, Walmart, ALDI, Lidl, Great Wall, H-Mart
Email results to the team only if something is worth buying.
Every trading day 9:00 AM Stock Market

Daily US Stock Radar

Before the market opens at 9 AM ET, the bot runs a technical screener on 15+ major stocks (AAPL, MSFT, NVDA, etc.), pulls fundamentals, ranks the top 3 strongest and bottom 3 riskiest, and posts the report to Telegram.

Prompt (what you tell the bot):
Run a pre-market stock scan. Check these tickers:
AAPL, MSFT, NVDA, AMZN, GOOGL, META, TSLA, ORCL, AVGO, INTC, TSM, AMD

For each stock, fetch:
1. Current price & RSI
2. Forward P/E & revenue growth
3. Technical trend (bullish / bearish / neutral)

Output:
✅ TOP 3 Strongest stocks with reasoning
⚠️ BOTTOM 3 Riskiest stocks with reasoning
Then a quick watchlist snapshot. Keep it concise.
Every day 10:00 AM Travel Deals

Cruise Deal Hunter

Scans Viking River Cruises' promotions page daily for Danube River sailings, looking for free airfare or reduced fare offers. If there's a deal, it emails the details automatically.

Prompt (what you tell the bot):
Check Viking River Cruises for Danube River promotions.
Look for: Free Airfare, Reduced Fare, Special Offers

For each offer found, include:
• Promotion name & details
• Price (if shown)
• Direct URL to the offer
• How long the offer is valid

Email the best offers. If nothing new, just check and say "no new deals."
Every day 11:00 AM Flight Search

Flight Price Monitor

Searches for flights from Washington D.C. to Taipei every day, checking price trends across multiple airlines. Sends a summary so you know when to buy.

Prompt (what you tell the bot):
Search for flights from Washington DC (IAD/DCA) to Taipei (TPE).

Find the cheapest options for each airline, then:
1. List the 3 cheapest round-trip flights
2. Note any price changes from yesterday
3. Flag if prices dropped more than 10%

Send a Telegram summary. No need to email unless there's a big drop.
Every morning 7:00 AM Personal Assistant

Daily Weather Briefing

Checks the weather for the day and emails a quick morning briefing — temperature, rain chance, and a recommendation (umbrella? jacket?).

Prompt (what you tell the bot):
Check today's weather in Fairfax, VA.

Tell me:
• High / Low temperature
• Rain / snow chance (%)
• Wind speed
• One-line verdict (rain jacket? short sleeves?)

Email it to the family. Keep it short and friendly.

Skills & Tools — How Agents Get Things Done

A bot without tools is just a fancy text generator. Skills give your bot superpowers — the ability to search the web, check stock prices, send emails, and more.

Web Search

Search Google for current info, prices, and deals.

Email

Send emails to anyone automatically.

Stock Data

Fetch real-time prices, RSI, P/E ratios.

Telegram Bot

Post messages, images, and reports to groups.

Weather

Check current weather and forecasts.

Custom Tools

Install new skills to extend your bot's abilities.

Pro tip: Skills are modular. Want your bot to track crypto prices? Install a crypto skill. Want it to generate images? Install an image skill. Mix and match for any use case.

3 Simple Prompts to Try Right Now

Just copy-paste any of these into your bot. No technical knowledge needed.

Prompt 1

"What's the weather today?"

Check the weather in my city and tell me if I need an umbrella or a jacket today.
Keep it brief — one sentence is fine.
Prompt 2

"Track a stock for me"

Check NVDA stock price and tell me if it's up or down today.
Show me price, RSI, and one sentence about the trend.
Prompt 3

"Send a daily digest to my group"

Every morning at 9:00 AM, post a quick market update to my Telegram group.
Include: S&P 500 change, top gainer, top loser, and today's key economic news.
Keep it under 5 bullet points.

Your First Bot in 3 Steps

1

Register your bot

Click "Create New Bot" on the dashboard. Give it a name and a platform (Telegram is the easiest to start with).

2

Write a simple prompt

Start with something easy like "Check the weather and post it to my Telegram group every morning." You can always change it later.

3

Set a schedule (or don't)

You can set a cron schedule like 0 9 * * 1-5 (every weekday at 9 AM) or leave it as a manual bot that only responds when you message it.
