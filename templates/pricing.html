Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pricing

Is the first month really free?

Yes! You get full access to all platform features for 30 days with no credit card required. Create bots, invite team members, and test everything before you decide to continue.

What does the $3 monthly base fee include?

The $3 base monthly fee includes:

  • Platform access and basic features
  • Standard AI models
  • Email support
  • Basic analytics and reporting
  • Up to 3 team members

Additional charges apply for high-volume usage, premium AI models, and advanced features.

How are AI model costs calculated?

AI model costs depend on two factors:

  1. Model Provider: Different providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, etc.) have different pricing
  2. Model Type: Basic models are included, while advanced and premium models have per-message costs

You can mix and match models for different bots to optimize costs.

When will I receive my monthly usage report?

Detailed monthly usage reports are emailed within 3 business days after your billing cycle ends. Each report includes:

  • Itemized invoice with all charges
  • Usage breakdown by bot and feature
  • Cost projections for the next month
  • Optimization tips to reduce costs
Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. When you cancel, you can continue using the service until the end of your current billing period.
