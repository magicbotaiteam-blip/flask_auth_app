Referral FAQ

How do I get my referral link?

Sign up for a free account, then visit the Referral Dashboard. Your unique link is shown at the top with copy and share buttons.

How many credits do I earn per referral?

1 credit per referral. When someone signs up using your link, you automatically receive 1 credit. There's no limit — refer as many people as you want!

What can I redeem credits for?

3 credits = 1 free month of service. Credits can also be used for premium features, advanced AI models, and more. More redemption options coming soon!

How do badge tiers work?

Every time you reach a new referral milestone, you unlock a higher badge tier. Each tier gives you bonus credits on top of the 1-per-referral credits you already earned. Track your progress on the referral dashboard.

Can I refer someone who's already a member?

If you refer someone who already has an account via email, you still earn the credit immediately! The system checks if the email is registered when you add a referral.
