Step 1: Create Your Account

Get started by creating your OpenClaw AI account

What You'll Need:
  • A valid email address
  • A secure password
  • Basic information about your use case
How to Sign Up:
1. Visit the Sign Up Page

Click on "Sign Up" in the navigation menu or visit /signup_local

2. Fill in Your Details

Enter your email, create a password, and provide basic information

3. Choose Authentication Method

Select between local authentication or Google OAuth for convenience

4. Verify Your Email

Check your inbox for a verification email and click the confirmation link

Pro Tip:

Use Google OAuth for faster signup and easier login. Your Google account is securely connected to OpenClaw AI.

Bot Creation Process:

Step 4: Find and Use Your Bot on Telegram

Connect with your bot after receiving the deployment notification

Notification Email:

Once your bot is ready, you'll receive an email with:

  • Bot username (e.g., @YourBotName)
  • Direct link to your bot
  • Quick start instructions
  • Support contact information
How to Find Your Bot on Telegram:
1. Open Telegram App

Launch Telegram on your mobile device or desktop

2. Search for Your Bot

Method A: Click the direct link from your email
Method B: Search for @YourBotName in Telegram search
Method C: Scan the QR code provided in email

3. Start Conversation

Click "Start" or send /start command to begin interacting with your bot

4. Test Your Bot

Send a message to verify your bot responds correctly

Mobile & Desktop Access:

Your bot works on all Telegram platforms:

iOS
Android
Desktop
Pro Tips for Telegram Bot Usage:
  • Pin the conversation for easy access
  • Create shortcuts on your home screen
  • Share your bot with team members
  • Monitor usage through your dashboard
Need Help?

If you can't find your bot or experience issues:

  • Check your spam folder for the notification email
  • Verify the bot username is correct
  • Contact support through your dashboard
  • Visit our FAQ section for common solutions

Step 5: Create Teams and Invite Members

Collaborate with your team on bot development and management

Team Management Process:
1. Create a New Team

From your dashboard, click "Create Team" and enter team details

2. Set Team Permissions

Admin: Full access to all features
Editor: Can modify bots but not delete
Viewer: Read-only access to monitor

3. Invite Team Members

Enter email addresses of team members to invite them

4. Assign Bots to Team

Select which bots this team can access and manage

Team Security Features:
  • Role-based access control
  • Audit logs for all changes
  • Two-factor authentication support
  • Session management per user
Collaboration Best Practices:

Create separate teams for different departments (e.g., Support Team, Development Team, Marketing Team) with appropriate permissions for each.
